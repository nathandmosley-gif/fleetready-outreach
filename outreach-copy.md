# FleetReady Index™ — Initial Outreach Copy

## Email

**Subject line options (A/B test these):**
1. Quick DOT compliance check for [Carrier Name]
2. [Carrier Name]'s FleetReady Score is ready
3. Is [Carrier Name] audit-ready? Here's a free score

**Body:**

```
Hi [First Name],

I ran a quick compliance scan on [Carrier Name] (DOT# [DOT_NUMBER]) using
FleetReady Index™ — it scores your carrier's audit readiness across the
factors FMCSA actually weighs during a review.

Your preliminary score is ready. It takes about 3 minutes to see your full
breakdown and where you stand vs. similar carriers in [State/Region].

[See Your FleetReady Score → LINK]

No cost, no obligation — just a clear picture of where you stand before
FMCSA finds out for you.

Reply YES and I'll send the link directly, or reply with any questions.

[Your Name]
FleetReady Index™
[Phone] | [Reply STOP to opt out]
```

**Notes:**
- Personalize DOT#, carrier name, and region — these are the biggest open-rate levers for cold carrier outreach.
- Keep it under 120 words. Fleet owners/safety managers skim on mobile.
- CAN-SPAM requires a physical mailing address in the footer and a working unsubscribe — add both.

## SMS

**Initial text (must include opt-out per TCPA):**

```
Hi [First Name], this is [Your Name] w/ FleetReady Index. I ran a quick
DOT compliance check for [Carrier Name] — want me to send your score?
Reply YES for the link or STOP to opt out.
```

Keep it at ~1 segment (160 chars) if possible — this one runs close, trim
[Carrier Name] to a short form if needed.

**Follow-up text if no reply after 2–3 days (optional second touch):**

```
Following up — your FleetReady score for [Carrier Name] is ready
whenever you want it: [LINK]. Reply STOP to opt out.
```

---

## Compliance notes (read before sending at volume)

- **TCPA**: cold SMS to carriers' business lines still needs prior express consent in many cases, or falls under limited exemptions — worth a quick legal check on your lead source before you scale texting. STOP/HELP keyword handling (built into Twilio) is required regardless.
- **CAN-SPAM**: physical address + unsubscribe link required in every email.
- Keep a **suppression list** synced between SMS and email so a STOP on one channel suppresses both.
